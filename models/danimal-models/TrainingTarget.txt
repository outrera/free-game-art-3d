Training target made for Open Dungeons (open source Dungeon keeper clone) and AgentKeeper (its brother-in-arms project) , 260 vertex, 229 faces, textured, with normal map, ambient oclussion(already applied to texture), specularity map and animated:

1-30   Right target rises
31-45 Right target goes down

46-75   Middle target rises
76-90 Middle target goes down

91-120   Left target rises
121-135 Left target goes down

138 All targets up - Still pose

All textures packed inside .blend;

Used Rusted metal textures done by Poss from here:

https://opengameart.org/content/rusted-metal-texture-pack  (CC-BY 3)
