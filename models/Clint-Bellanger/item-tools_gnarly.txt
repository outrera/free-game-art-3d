A set of gnarly medieval tools in 3D. Created in Blender.

Comes with one texture that works for all the tools.

Very low polygon. Each one is around 100 faces.

Hatchet
Sickle
Kama
Sledge Hammer
Shovel
Pitchfork
Hoe
Scythe
Pickaxe
Heavy Axe
