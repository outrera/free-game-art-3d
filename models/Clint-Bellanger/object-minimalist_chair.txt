A simple minimalist wood chair.

Tech notes:

Manifold, low poly (316 tri and 172 tris versions), edge split.
256x256 wood diffuse texture (public domain from the Blender Texture CD) packed
I created it flat, unwrapped it, then curved the wood into place.
