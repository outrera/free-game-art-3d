Simple isometric flagstone floors.  32 unique tiles of size 64x32.

Includes tiles with cracks to add a nice subtle variance.

The .zip includes the Blender file used to create these. Tutorial on how I make these kind of tiles: http://clintbellanger.net/rpg/tutorials/isometric_tiles/
