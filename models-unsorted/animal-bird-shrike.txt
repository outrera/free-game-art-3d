Borked workflow test for feathers and getting a bird model under 1k tris. Works as a background prop.

The .zip file includes the .blend with the scene and lighting setup used to make the preview.

Models:

Lowpoly mesh, IK rigged, UVed (996 tris)

Textures (2K):

Body Diffuse + Alpha
Body Occlusion
Body Normal + Bump
Body Roughness (256^2)
Body SSS/Translucent (256^2)
